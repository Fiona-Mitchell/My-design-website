Ingrid Aaroe's Ceramics

Ingrid is a Scandinavian-American ceramist. She specialises in handmade functional ceramics. Her utilitarian approach to design is strongly influenced by the Japanese and Scandinavian aesthetic; she spent many years residing in Japan and Hong Kong, and is now based in London. In 2018 she took part in Kew's Handmade in Britain and works from her local studio in South-West London.


Ingrid uses her website as a way to expand her online presence. To build this website I forked the aaroeceramics.github.io repository. I wanted to give the website a fresh, minimalist feel to highlight the aesthetic of the ceramics. To begin, I designed and placed her logo at the head of every page. Her main pages were: landing page, portfolio page, and about page. I created a simple navigation menu which remains consistent throughout the site. I used flex-box to re-design her about page and make the site mobile-friendly. Her portfolio is displayed as a simple scroll-gallery, as each of her pieces are unique.
