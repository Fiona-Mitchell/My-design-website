Connect Educate - Hackathon

Connect Educate is an open source app developed at the #UKvsCovidHack. Its aim is to connect isolated parents with community mentors.


Due to the sudden stay-at-home isolation, parents are overwhelmed with the immense variety of education and entertainment resources across Google Classroom, Microsoft 365, Zoom YouTube and online parenting groups. In response to this, WDD (Women Driven Development) is seeking to solve some of the challenges that have arisen due to this rapid introduction of technology.


Frontline key workers and charity representatives were invited to talk about the difficulties they and their communities are facing in the battle against COVID-19 during our pre-hack. There were livestream presentations on public health, food banks, women and family services, education, homelessness and accessibility.
