Nice to meet you -

I'm Fiona, a designer specialising in fashion & digital experience.

I am available for fashion and digital freelance projects, collaborations and illustration commissions.

In addition to freelance practice, I work as a Tailor by day at Levi Strauss, London. Prior to this position, I led accessory design and creative direction at OSIER. I have been designing for over five years, from typography projects to catwalk collections. In May 2018 I graduated from The University of Edinburgh, UK, with a 1st class honours degree in Fashion Design.
