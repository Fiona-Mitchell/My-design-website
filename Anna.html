Anna's Fitness Website

Anna is an exercise lover and plant based foodie who’s passionate about promoting a balanced, body positive & healthy approach to life. She is based in London and offers personalised training sessions for all abilities. The majority of her clients reach her through her social media or via email. Her previous website included many different pages with a lack of connection between them. When designing Anna’s website, I wanted a sleek and simple layout. When her clients come to her website they have three main objectives:

  1. Her services
  2. Her prices
  3. How to contact

I designed and built a one-page portfolio site to outline the main sections of her site: about, services, lifestyle, blog and contact. Underneath the main menu I added in-page links to important sections on the page. I used a blue-green colour scheme, in keeping with her original logo colour scheme. Each section is separated with a dotted border, and location/contact sections with a moss-green background colour.
