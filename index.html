Nice to meet you -


I'm Fiona, a digital designer and junior developer specialising in fashion & digital experience.

I am passionate about digital retail experiences and digital fashion design.

While I'm not coding, I work as a Made-to-order Tailor by day at Levi Strauss, London. Prior to this position, I led accessory design and creative direction at OSIER. I have been designing for over five years, from typography projects to catwalk collections. In May 2018 I graduated from The University of Edinburgh, UK, with a 1st class honours degree in Fashion Design. I am currently pursuing my MSc in the digital realm, focusing on creative programming and user experience.
